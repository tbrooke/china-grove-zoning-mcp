Please consider this request for leave as I have been called upon for military duty. I request this leave for
the duration of this duty.

I understand that I am obligated to return to work at the end of this leave. Upon returning to work, I
understand that I am entitled to return to the same position held at the time leave was granted or to one of
like classification, seniority, pay, and level of leave accrual. If I decide not to return to work, I realize that
I must notify my supervisor immediately. I understand that failure to request re-employment as required by
law may be considered a resignation. I understand that the Town will make up any difference between my
